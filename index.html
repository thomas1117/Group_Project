Founded in 2015 by three individuals who love to watch netflix and chicken came across a coding project in where they had to create a website for an ‘imaginary restaurant’ . So we thought, what idea would be better than merging the two things we like most : chicken and Orange is the New Black. Orange is the New Chicken is the a jail themed restaurant in where you walk in going through a metal detector and see the crew dressed up as prison guards and you are the new inmate. We serve chicken based on the cast of OITNB the different styles in different styles to meet your different cravings.
